FEE STRUCTURE

Payment process:

Internet Banking: You may pay the fee online.

Payment through Debit/Credit cards : You can also make payment through Debit/Credit Cards.

Payment through Demand Draft: Demand Draft should be in favour of "IIIT VADODARA" for Institute fee

Payment through Demand Draft: Demand Draft should be in favour of "IIITV Mess Committee" for Mess fee

Bankdetails

No cash payment will be accepted.

Note: Being an IT Institute it is desired that dues to the Institute are remitted through available e-modes viz. net banking and debit/credit cards. An additional amount of Rs.500/- will be applicable for payment through non e-mode, as handling charge.
