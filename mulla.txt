          <b>Dr. R Nagaraj</b><br>
<b>Director DA-IICT,</b><br>
<b>Mentor Director, IIITV</b><br><br>
            
            <p>
Dr. R Nagaraj obtained his PhD from Visvesvaraya Technological University in the field of Fault Tolerant Flight Controllers using Artificial Neural Network (ANN).
 His areas of interest include fault tolerant control, Non linear control systems, neural networks and reconfigurable control systems.</p>
<p> Dr. Nagaraj has twenty five years of experience in research, teaching and academic administration. </p>
<p> Prior to joining DA-IICT, he was the Principal of The Oxford College of Engineering; Bangalore where he held positions of Vice-Principal, Professor and Head of the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering.</p>
<p> He was the national merit scholar and received academic excellence award from RSST and mentorship award from RV VLSI Design Center, Bangalore.</p>
<p> Dr. Nagaraj is IEEE Senior Member, Life Member of Institution of Electronics & Telecommunication Engineers, and Indian Society for Technical Education and Computer Society of India.</p><br><br>
